# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Independent video creators and educators who have finished video content and want to make it accessible to audiences in additional languages.

## Product Purpose

Dubzy helps creators reach new-language audiences quickly, produce professional localized videos, and reduce the time and cost of dubbing.

## Positioning

Dubzy gives independent creators and educators a simple, affordable way to turn one finished video into professional multilingual versions.

## Brand Personality

Capable, clear, and trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Dubzy should not resemble a generic AI SaaS template, an intimidating professional editing suite, or an overly playful consumer app.

## Design Principles

- Keep the path from finished video to localized version direct and understandable.
- Make professional capability feel approachable to independent creators and educators.
- Communicate progress, status, and outcomes clearly enough to earn trust.
- Support both the public brand experience and the authenticated workflow without losing a coherent identity.
- Let the interface serve the creator's work instead of competing with it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific accessibility standard or audience accommodation has been requested.
